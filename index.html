Measurement Guide

  • 1 Chest — measure around the fullest part
  • 2 Shoulder — across from shoulder to shoulder
  • 3 Waist — around the narrowest part
  • 4 Sleeve Length — shoulder tip to wrist
  • 5 Length — shoulder to desired hem
